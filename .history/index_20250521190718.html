Anvil Grid

What services does Anvil Grid provide?

We ensure seamless integration, efficiency, and reliability in power distribution networks by providing turnkey solutions for Advanced Distribution Management Systems (ADMS), SCADA, Outage Management Systems (OMS), Distribution Management Systems (DMS), Distributed Energy Resource Management Systems (DERMS), Substation Automation, and Smart Distribution Infrastructure.

What is a smart meter?

A smart meter is a digital electricity meter that tracks your electricity usage, gives accurate bills, and supports prepaid or postpaid plans, having two-way communication. If you have a solar system installed at your place, it measures the difference between the electricity you send to the grid and what you take from it, so you only pay for what you use.

What is SCADA?

SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) is a software and hardware system used in the power sector to monitor and control electricity grids in real-time. It helps ensure efficient power distribution, quick fault detection, and smooth grid operations.

What are the benefits of switching to solar power?
  • Significantly reduced electricity bills
  • Uninterrupted power supply (hybrid solar system)
  • Low-cost maintenance
  • 20 years of warranty on solar panel module
  • Low carbon footprint
Does Anvil Urja handle installation and maintenance?

Yes, we provide turnkey solar solutions. We take care of all the hassles of installation, paperwork, and post-sales maintenance.

How to install a solar system at my home?

All you have to do is give a missed call to your Anvil Saathi at the toll-free number 1800 2020 001. He will come to your home and give you a free consultation. Our team of trained professionals will then install a solar system on your rooftop, customised to your needs.

Do solar panels work during cloudy or rainy days?

Solar panels continue to generate electricity on cloudy or rainy days, although at a reduced output. Any shortfall of electricity can be easily compensated by drawing power from the grid.

Is there a government subsidy on solar plants? How can I apply for the government subsidy?

Yes, the Government of India offers subsidies under the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) for residential and institutional installations. The subsidy amount can cover up to 30% of the benchmark cost.

You can visit the website www.pmsuryaghar.gov.in to self-register or you can just give a missed call to your Anvil Saathi at the toll-free number 1800 2020 001. He will handle all the paperwork and process on your behalf.

Does Anvil have any tie-ups with banks for financing rooftop solar?

We facilitate easy financing for our customers through multiple banks that are associated with the PM Surya Ghar Yojana. We take care of end-to-end document processing, thereby ensuring a hassle free solar switch for you. You would need to select “Anvil Energy Pvt Ltd.” as your preferred vendor on the PMSGY portal, and let the experts at Anvil take care of the rest.
